# GodSaveUsAll - Multiplayer Game Documentation

## Overview
GodSaveUsAll is an immersive multiplayer game that invites players into a rich virtual world full of challenges and adventures. Players must collaborate, strategize, and compete to achieve victory.

## Features
- **Multiplayer Mode**: Engage with friends or players worldwide.
- **Dynamic Environments**: Explore various landscapes and biomes.
- **Customizable Characters**: Personalize your gameplay experience.
- **In-Game Currency**: Earn and spend currency on items and upgrades.
- **Regular Updates**: Enjoy fresh content and new features regularly.
  
## How to Play
### Getting Started
1. Download and install GodSaveUsAll from the official website.
2. Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.
3. Choose your character and customize it to your liking.
4. Join a game lobby or create your own to invite friends.

### Game Objectives
- Complete missions to earn rewards.
- Compete against other players to dominate the leaderboard.
- Collaborate with your team to achieve common goals.

## Controls
- **W, A, S, D**: Move your character.
- **Spacebar**: Jump.
- **Left Click**: Attack or interact.
- **Right Click**: Use special abilities.
- **Esc**: Access the game menu.

## Network Protocol
The game uses a client-server model for communication:
- **TCP/IP Protocol**: Reliable connection ensuring data packet delivery.
- **UDP for Gameplay Data**: Faster transmission for real-time actions.
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted is secured to protect player information.

## Troubleshooting
- **Connection Issues**: Ensure your internet is stable. Try rebooting your router.
- **Game Crashes**: Update your graphics drivers and ensure your system meets the game specifications.
- **Latency Problems**: Consider switching to a server closer to your geographical location.
